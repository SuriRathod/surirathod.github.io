About

Engineer.
Analyst. Implementer.

I'm an SAP Business Analyst with a background that most consultants don't have — I've actually worked the floor. My foundation in Chemical Engineering, sharpened by an MBA and a Master's in Industrial & Systems Engineering from Ohio State, means I understand why processes exist before I digitize them.

I specialize in SAP S/4HANA implementations for manufacturing environments, with deep focus on the MM (Materials Management) and PP (Production Planning) modules. I've led go-live deployments at plant facilities — from process mapping and data source identification through user training and post-go-live support.

Outside of SAP, I write about ERP strategy in manufacturing, build Python tools and web applications, and believe that the best solutions come from people who can talk to both the operator on the floor and the architect in the boardroom.

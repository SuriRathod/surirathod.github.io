About

Engineer.
Analyst. Implementer.

I'm a Manufacturing Systems Analyst and SAP S/4HANA implementer at HP Hood LLC — one of North America's largest dairy and food companies. My background isn't typical for an ERP professional: I started with a B.Tech in Chemical Engineering, earned an MBA in Operations Strategy from NMIMS, and then a Master's in Industrial & Systems Engineering from Ohio State — because I wanted to understand operations at every level before digitizing them.

Over the past 5+ years at HP Hood, I've progressed from driving Lean and Six Sigma initiatives as a Continuous Improvement Specialist to leading SAP S/4HANA plant implementations — handling everything from process mapping and data source identification to user training, quality testing, and post-go-live support across multiple manufacturing facilities.

I specialize in the MM (Materials Management) and PP (Production Planning) modules, with hands-on experience in transactions including MIGO, COR1/2/3, BMBC, and MI10. Outside of SAP, I enjoy building data tools in Python, and believe the best digital transformations are led by people who truly understand what happens on the plant floor.
